Useful Symbols

  • nbsp; - Non-breaking space (like a space that won't break into a new line)
  • copy; - Copyright symbol (©)
  • euro; - Euro symbol (€)

Why Use Them?

  • To show special characters like <, >, & in your page.
  • To add cool symbols (like ©, €, ™).
  • To make sure your HTML works everywhere!
